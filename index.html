Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 June 2026

AC Universal Remote Control ("the app", "we", "us") is a universal infrared (IR) remote control for air conditioners, TVs and similar devices. This policy explains what data the app and its service providers handle, and your choices.

In short:
  • The app needs no account and no login, and your saved remotes work offline.
  • The free version shows ads via Google AdMob, which collects device and advertising identifiers to serve and measure them.
  • An optional subscription removes all ads. Payments are handled by Google Play; we never see your card details.
  • Your saved remotes and settings are stored only on your device.

Information collected

By the app: We do not ask you to create an account and do not collect your name, contacts, or precise location. The remotes you add and their settings are stored locally on your device (see below).

By Google AdMob (free version only): To show ads, Google AdMob and its partners may collect and process:

  • advertising/device identifiers (e.g. Android Advertising ID),
  • IP address and coarse, IP-derived location,
  • device information (model, operating system, language), and
  • ad-interaction and app-usage data.

This is used to serve, cap and measure ads and to prevent fraud. Where required (e.g. the EEA, UK), ads are personalized only with your consent, gathered through Google's consent form; otherwise non-personalized ads are shown. You can change your choice anytime via Settings → Privacy options inside the app. Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from apps that use its services.

By Google Play (purchases): If you start the subscription or free trial, Google Play processes the payment and may share with us your purchase status and a purchase token so we can unlock the ad-free experience. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

Data stored on your device

The remotes you add and their last-used settings (temperature, mode, fan, etc.) are saved locally using standard app storage. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app.

Permissions

  • Transmit IR (android.permission.TRANSMIT_IR) — to send infrared signals from your phone's IR blaster to your devices.
  • Vibrate — for button feedback.
  • Internet / Billing — used by the ads service (free version) and to process the optional subscription. Core remote features work without a connection.

How the data is used

To operate the app, display and measure ads in the free version, process the optional subscription, prevent fraud/abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data described above is shared only with the service providers that make those features work — Google AdMob (ads) and Google Play Billing (purchases) — acting under Google's terms and privacy policy.

Your choices and rights

  • Remove ads: subscribe to remove all ads and the associated ad data collection.
  • Reset/limit ad tracking: Android Settings → Privacy → Ads lets you reset or delete your advertising ID and opt out of personalization.
  • Manage subscription: Google Play → Payments & subscriptions.
  • Consent: change your ads-consent choice in Settings → Privacy options.
  • GDPR (EEA/UK) & CCPA/CPRA (US): you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data, and to opt out of personalized advertising. Contact us to exercise them.

Children's privacy

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data retention

On-device data persists until you delete it or uninstall the app. Data processed by Google for ads and payments is retained according to Google's policies.

International transfers

Our service providers (Google) may process data on servers located in other countries under appropriate safeguards.

Changes to this policy

If we change how data is handled, we will update this page and the in-app/store disclosures before those changes take effect.
